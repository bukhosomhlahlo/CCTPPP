City Park Renovation

Project Overview

The City of Cape Town is embarking on an exciting initiative to renovate and revitalize one of our beloved public parks. The goal of this project is to enhance recreational opportunities, preserve natural beauty, and create a welcoming space that reflects the needs and aspirations of our diverse community.

Why Your Input Matters:

Your input is essential to the success of this project. We believe that by working together, we can design a park that serves as a vibrant gathering place for residents of all ages and backgrounds. Your ideas, preferences, and feedback will help shape the future of our community green space.
